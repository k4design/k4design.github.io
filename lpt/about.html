Our Story

LPT Realty exists to solve a fundamental problem in real estate: agents need more than a brokerage—they need a platform that amplifies their success. We built LPT Realty to bridge the gap between traditional brokerages and the modern tools agents need to compete and win.

Our structure is designed around agent autonomy and client outcomes. We provide the marketing systems, operational support, and technology infrastructure that let agents focus on what they do best—building relationships and closing deals. Every decision we make is filtered through one question: does this help our agents serve their clients better?

The result is a brokerage that scales with intention. We support agents at every stage, from onboarding to transaction management to business growth. This isn't just about real estate—it's about building a sustainable, profitable business that elevates the entire industry.
