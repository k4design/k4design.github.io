About the Dezzy Awards

The Dezzy Awards were established to recognize and celebrate the extraordinary achievements of LP Realty's finest real estate professionals. Named after our commitment to excellence, these awards represent the pinnacle of recognition within our organization.

Each year, we gather to honor those who have demonstrated exceptional dedication, innovation, and results. From record-breaking sales to outstanding client service, the Dezzy Awards shine a spotlight on the individuals who elevate our industry standards.

More than just recognition, the Dezzy Awards embody our core values: integrity, excellence, and community. Winners are selected through a rigorous evaluation process that considers not only performance metrics but also peer nominations and client testimonials.
