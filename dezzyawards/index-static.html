About the Dezzy Awards

One of the biggest challenges in a cloud-based national brokerage is agent recognition. As growth accelerates, individual wins can get lost — especially when success is measured only at the national or state level. Brick-and-mortar offices have long held one advantage here: the geographic density to recognize excellence at a hyper-local level in a way that hasn't historically been possible at national scale. That never sat right with us.

At LPT Realty, we know the hard work happens locally — ZIP code by ZIP code, city by city, market by market. And that belief led us to build something entirely new. We're proud to introduce the Dezzy Awards — a first-of-its-kind recognition program designed to celebrate top-performing agents from coast to coast, at the most meaningful level: their real markets.

A New Standard for Agent Recognition

The Dezzy Awards recognize excellence not just at national and state levels, but across cities and ZIP codes, creating more than 2,000 opportunities for agents to be acknowledged for their performance — without competing against uneven markets or unrealistic benchmarks. That context matters deeply to us as champions of each agent's individual definition of success. With the Dezzy awards, we want success to feel attainable, relevant, and real — just as it should be.

And the recognition won't stop inside our LPT Community. Top honorees will be featured in The Wall Street Journal, with additional recognitions published in select local newspapers, giving recipients broader industry visibility, market credibility, and a meaningful brand lift in their local communities.
