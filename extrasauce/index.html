The Lore

From a
Brooklyn alley...
to Wall Street…
and back.

We started on a folding table in Brooklyn with a screaming-hot griddle and zero business plan — just beef smashed thin until the edges went lacy, gold, and frankly illegal.

The line got long, the internet got loud, and here we are. Same beef, same sauce, same chip on our shoulder — now with a Wall Street counter and Bushwick loading next.
