The Origin

Born in a Brooklyn pop-up. Raised on attitude.

We started slinging smash burgers out of a pop-up on the streets of Brooklyn — no frills, no shortcuts, just screaming-hot griddles and beef smashed thin until the edges go lacy and gold.

That cult following followed us downtown. Now we're bringing the borough's energy and our obsession with quality ingredients straight to Wall Street — with Bushwick loading next.
