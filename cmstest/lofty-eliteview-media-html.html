Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our luxury real estate media platform.

What is addressable TV/CTV and why is it different?
Addressable TV and CTV allow us to deliver different ads to different households watching the same program, based on their specific demographics, interests, and behaviors. This precision targeting eliminates waste and ensures your luxury real estate message reaches only qualified prospects.
How do you verify HNW/UHNW audiences?
We use privacy-safe data partners and sophisticated modeling to identify high-net-worth households through income proxies, property values, lifestyle indicators, and purchasing behaviors. All data is anonymized and compliant with privacy regulations.
What measurement do we receive?
Our comprehensive measurement includes incremental reach, view-through rates, website visits, high-intent actions, and full attribution tracking. We provide detailed reporting on campaign performance and audience engagement.
How fast can we launch?
Typical launch timeline is 2-3 weeks from contract signing, including audience modeling, creative adaptation, and inventory placement. Rush launches can be accommodated for urgent campaigns.
Do you offer market exclusivity?
Yes, our Signature tier includes regional exclusivity, while Prestige and Black Label tiers offer broader market protection. We ensure your luxury real estate brand maintains its premium positioning.
Do you manage creative?
Our Prestige and Black Label tiers include creative concierge services, helping optimize your luxury real estate creative for maximum impact across CTV and audio platforms. We work with your existing creative or develop new assets.
