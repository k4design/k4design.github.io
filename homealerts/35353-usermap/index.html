Three design directions

The consumer-facing marketing surface is being explored as three parallel looks — not a sequence of upgrades. Pick one and the tiles below update. They share the 35353 product, copy spine, and coral accent; they differ in palette, type, layout, and tone.

V1 · LIGHT
Classic
Mood
Bright, friendly, familiar PropTech SaaS. The safe, high-trust default.
Palette
White / near-white base, coral #f37655 + teal #006a64 on dark ink #1a1a1a.
Type
Plus Jakarta Sans throughout — clean geometric sans, bold headlines.
Layout
Centered hero + phone mockup, stat bar, “text vs QR” comparison, feature cards, teal CTA band.
Best for
Maximum legibility & broad appeal; lowest-risk conversion look.
/
Bold cinematic
V2 · DARK
Mood
Premium, high-energy, app-startup confidence. Most distinctive.
Palette
Near-black canvas #15110e, warm off-white text #f6efe8, coral #f37655 as the single hot accent over full-bleed photography.
Type
Plus Jakarta Sans + Hanken Grotesk; oversized expressive display headers.
Layout
Cinematic dark sections, interactive phone-demo hero, “every sign rider becomes a lead” coral panel.
Watch-out
Dark contrast is hardest to read for 45–65 agents & in bright curbside sun (reviewed 7.5/10).
/v2
V3 · WARM EDITORIAL
Warm
Mood
Warm, human, editorial. Boldness of V2 tempered for readability.
Palette
Warm paper #f7f1ea, high-contrast warm ink #241c16 (legibility-first), coral accent, one espresso anchor #211a15.
Type
Fraunces display serif paired with Jakarta/Hanken — magazine voice.
Layout
Serif headlines, “three steps” numbered story, espresso “real person” section with team photos.
Best for
Relationship-led positioning that keeps strong contrast outdoors.

How users move through it.

— Buyer journey

From sign to showing

01
Sees yard sign, billboard, or hears radio spot for 35353
02
Texts the property code to the agent's number
03
Receives SMS reply with link to /p/:code
04
Lands on the public listing page (mobile)
05
Taps Schedule a Showing → submits form
— Agent acquisition

From visitor to first listing

01
Lands on Home or For Agents from organic / ads
02
Reviews Pricing and Integrations
03
Clicks Register → fills 3-step form
04
Lands in Dashboard, takes the onboarding tour
05
Creates first listing, gets the code, prints the sign
— Agent daily

Working the pipeline

01
Signs in → Dashboard for the morning KPI scan
02
Reviews new leads in Prospects
03
Replies via Lead detail conversation thread
04
Books appointments in Showings calendar
05
Reviews trends in Analytics weekly

Audits Users and Teams as needed
