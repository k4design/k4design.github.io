About Sarah Mitchell

Sarah Mitchell is a distinguished Senior Luxury Real Estate Specialist with over 15 years of experience in Manhattan's most prestigious neighborhoods. Her unparalleled expertise in luxury penthouses, historic properties, and investment sales has earned her recognition as one of New York's top-performing agents.

Sarah's deep understanding of Manhattan's luxury market, combined with her exceptional negotiation skills and attention to detail, has resulted in over $2.1 billion in total sales volume. She specializes in representing both domestic and international clients seeking the finest properties in Manhattan, from iconic pre-war buildings to cutting-edge new developments.

Her approach is characterized by discretion, professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to achieving her clients' goals. Sarah's extensive network of industry professionals, including architects, designers, and financial advisors, ensures that her clients receive comprehensive support throughout their real estate journey.

Professional Philosophy

"Luxury real estate is not just about the property—it's about understanding the lifestyle and aspirations of each client. I believe in building lasting relationships based on trust, integrity, and exceptional service. Every transaction is an opportunity to exceed expectations and create extraordinary experiences."

Market Expertise

Sarah has extensive experience in Manhattan's most coveted neighborhoods, including the Upper East Side, Upper West Side, Tribeca, SoHo, and Midtown. She has successfully represented clients in landmark buildings such as 15 Central Park West, 432 Park Avenue, and One57, among others.
