CHAPTER II: THE CLARITY

What is a
rate-and-term refinance?

Refining the deal

Think of it as an upgrade for your current mortgage. You replace your existing loan with a new one that features a better interest rate or a more favorable timeline.

Monthly vs. Long-term

You aren't cashing out your equity. You're simply making your money work harder—either by lowering your monthly bill or paying off your home years earlier.
