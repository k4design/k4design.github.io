APERTURE LEADERSHIP

Robert Palmer

Founder/CEO

Robert Palmer is a serial entrepreneur, business leader and marketing guru who has built an impressive portfolio of brands and businesses through innovation, hard work, and a relentless passion for success. In 2022, Palmer launched LPT Realty, the fastest growing single-entity national brokerage in history, designed to empower agents through an innovative model that focuses on agent choice. Seeing a hole in the industry for truly global and effective marketing for luxury real estate, he launched Aperture in 2025, further disrupting the industry.

Michael Valdes

Global President

Michael Valdes is a leader in luxury real estate, serving as Global President of Aperture and CEO of LPT International. Prior to this role, he served as Chief Growth Officer of exp Realty and former Senior Vice President of Realogy Corporation where he spent 15 years at Sotheby's Realty growing that brand to over 70 countries.  Prior to real estate, Valdes spent 10 years at Deutsche Bank Private Banking. 

Mercedes Saewitz

SVP of Operations

A Miami native with over 20 years of industry experience, Mercedes Saewitz is a recognized leader in real estate. During her decade at Compass, she led regional brokerage operations, managed top-producing teams, and drove national initiatives, fostering innovation and excellence. Passionate about agent growth and innovation, she specializes in streamlining processes, implementing strategic solutions, and empowering agents to excel in the luxury market 

Matt Hodge

Executive Vice President

Matthew Hodge’s real estate journey began with a deep passion for helping individuals build wealth through real estate. This commitment to client success paved the way for a seamless transition into luxury sales. Today, Hodge leverages his expertise and leadership to empower agents to grow and scale their businesses effectively through the innovative LPT platform.

Michael Valdes

Global President
