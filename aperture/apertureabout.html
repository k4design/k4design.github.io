Robert Palmer

Founder/CEO

Robert Palmer is a serial entrepreneur, business leader and marketing guru who has built an impressive portfolio of brands and businesses through innovation, hard work, and a relentless passion for success. In 2022, Palmer launched LPT Realty, the fastest growing single-entity national brokerage in history, designed to empower agents through an innovative model that focuses on agent choice. Seeing a hole in the industry for truly global and effective marketing for luxury real estate, he launched Aperture in 2025, further disrupting the industry.

Michael Valdes

Global President

Michael Valdes is a global leader in luxury real estate, serving as Global President of Aperture and CEO of LPT International. With a track record of opening 23 countries in 22 months at eXp Realty and overseeing global servicing for Realogy’s luxury brands like Sotheby’s International Realty, Valdes brings visionary leadership to the industry. He also hosts "The Michael Valdes Global Podcast," reaching over 100 countries and 12 million impressions.
