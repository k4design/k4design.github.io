Architectural Elegance & Interior Design

The villas showcase a seamless fusion of modern minimalism and Caribbean charm. Expansive open-plan layouts are complemented by floor-to-ceiling windows, allowing natural light to illuminate the interiors adorned with Elie Saab Maison furnishings. Lightweight materials, airy textures, and hues inspired by the island’s landscapes create an ambiance of understated luxury. Each residence features a private glass elevator, a state-of-the-art kitchen equipped with Gaggenau appliances, and bespoke finishes that epitomize sophistication.

Outdoor Living & Amenities

Emphasizing the allure of outdoor living, each villa boasts a heated infinity pool and jacuzzi, expansive terraces, and lush private gardens. The design ensures unobstructed views of the Caribbean Sea, providing a serene backdrop for relaxation and entertainment. Smart home technology by Control4 offers seamless integration of lighting, climate, and security systems, enhancing the living experience. Additional features include a two-car garage and 24/7 concierge services, ensuring convenience and peace of mind.

Indigo Bay Community

Situated within the exclusive Indigo Bay enclave, residents enjoy a harmonious blend of privacy and accessibility. The gated community offers 24-hour security and is strategically located just minutes from Princess Juliana International Airport, Philipsburg, and Marigot. The area is renowned for its pristine beaches, vibrant markets, and a fusion of Dutch and French cultural influences. Future developments include a five-star resort, enhancing the community’s appeal and investment potential.

Founded by Elie Saab in 1982, ELIE SAAB was the first Lebanese couture house to be recognized by Camera Nazionale della Moda in Rome (1997) and was among the first international members admitted to the prestigious Chambre Syndicale (2003). Known for its exceptional savoir-faire and timeless appeal, ELIE SAAB is recognized as one of the most significant couture houses at the forefront of fashion, sought out by celebrities, royalty, and public figures alike.

With ateliers in Paris, Milan, London, and Lebanon, and over one hundred boutiques worldwide, the label continues to grow into one of the world’s leading luxury lifestyle brands—offering diversified lines, from ready-to-wear and bridal to accessories, eyewear, children’s wear, watches, and fragrances. ELIE SAAB recently established MAISON (2019), with signature furniture collections and exclusive interiors for global real estate ventures.
