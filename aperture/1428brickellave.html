A VERTICAL MASTERPIECE: UNRIVALED INNOVATION

Rising 70 stories above the Miami skyline, this iconic tower is a limited collection of just 189 exclusive residences, offering unparalleled luxury and privacy. As the world’s first high-rise residential building partially powered by the sun, its striking solar backbone on the west façade sets a new standard in sustainable living. Residents enjoy 80,000 square feet of private amenities, high-speed elevators, and a dedicated lobby, all within a fully gated, resident-only enclave. Each home includes valet-serviced parking with private loading bays, EV charging stations, and superior soundproofing through enhanced concrete wall construction—ensuring both comfort and serenity in the heart of the city.

Founded by Yamal Yidios in 2006, Ytech is a Miami-based real estate development firm renowned for its design-centric approach and commitment to innovation. The company has established a significant presence across 25 markets in the southeastern United States, developing and owning over 10 million square feet of property.

Ytech’s portfolio includes landmark projects such as The Residences at 1428 Brickell, a 70-story luxury condominium tower in Miami’s Brickell Financial District. This development is notable for being the first high-rise residential tower to integrate photovoltaic glazing within its façade, creating a "Solar Backbone" that generates clean energy while setting a new benchmark in sustainable architecture. Designed by Arquitectonica with interiors by ACPV ARCHITECTS, the tower features 195 exclusive residences and over 80,000 square feet of private amenities.

With a development pipeline exceeding $3 billion in luxury condominium projects within Brickell alone, Ytech continues to redefine urban living through superior design, exceptional craftsmanship, and a relentless pursuit of meaningful innovation.
