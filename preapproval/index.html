Mortgage Glossary

DTI — Debt-to-Income Ratio
The share of your gross monthly income that goes toward paying debts. Lenders want this below 43%.
PMI — Private Mortgage Insurance
Required when you put down less than 20%. Typically 0.5–1.5% of the loan per year.
Escrow
A neutral third-party account that holds funds for taxes and insurance until they're due.
APR — Annual Percentage Rate
The true borrowing cost — interest rate plus all lender fees expressed as a yearly percentage.
