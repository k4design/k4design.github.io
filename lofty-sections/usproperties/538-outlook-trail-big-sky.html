The Story

538 Outlook Trail is a mountain-modern retreat that delivers effortless ski-in/ski-out access at Spanish Peaks Mountain Club's Highlands—one of Big Sky's most exclusive private communities. A ski trail just across the street connects directly to the Highlands Chairlift, placing world-class terrain steps from the front door. Built in 2021 and finished throughout by a talented local designer, the home strikes the balance between alpine warmth and contemporary refinement.

The main level unfolds in an open-concept kitchen, dining, and living arrangement flooded with natural light from abundant windows framing the mountain views. A lower-level family room with wet bar provides a second gathering space ideal for après-ski. Multiple outdoor decks extend the living area into the landscape, while a private hot tub offers a setting for stargazing after a day on the mountain.

Membership at Spanish Peaks Mountain Club is required upon closing, unlocking access to two championship golf courses, tennis and pickleball courts, fitness centers, pools, and clubhouse dining—plus the soon-to-open Aspire Lodge, a 28,000 sq ft facility adding a restaurant, expanded fitness, and recreational spaces.
