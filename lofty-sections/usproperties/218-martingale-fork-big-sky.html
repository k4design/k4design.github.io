The Story

218 Martingale Fork #13 is a singular opportunity within one of Big Sky's most exclusive and constrained offerings: one of only 15 private Mountain Home residences inside the gates of Spanish Peaks Mountain Club. This front-row position within the Montage Big Sky collection places the residence at the convergence of the most coveted amenities in the market—steps from the Montage Big Sky hotel, the Spanish Peaks Clubhouse, the driving range, and the Tom Weiskopf Signature Golf Course.

Classic Montana character is delivered at every turn: large timber beams, natural wood throughout, expansive wood-burning fireplaces in both the main living area and lower level, a gas fireplace in the master bedroom, and vaulted ceilings in the main living room and master suite. The outdoor deck features its own fireplace, and a hot tub on the lower-level patio extends the living experience through every season.

Ski-in/ski-out access via the Cabin Lift is a defining feature—as is the full suite of Montage lifestyle privileges and hotel amenities available to Mountain Home owners. With six bedrooms across 5,515 sq ft and HOA dues covering professional management, this is the ideal primary or secondary residence for those who demand the best of mountain Montana.
