The Story

218 Martingale Fork #13 is a rare front-row offering within the ultra-exclusive Montage Big Sky Mountain Homes—one of only 15 private residences inside the prestigious gates of Spanish Peaks Mountain Club. Ideally positioned with unobstructed views across protected open space and the #1 fairway of the Weiskopf Signature Golf Course, this fully furnished, turn-key residence is among the most coveted settings in Big Sky.

The home blends timeless alpine architecture with refined mountain-modern finishes. The open-concept great room—anchored by a dramatic stone fireplace—flows seamlessly into a chef's kitchen with Wolf and Sub-Zero appliances and an oversized island. Three indoor fireplaces, an outdoor firepit, and an outdoor hot tub extend the living experience through every season. Six guest suites with spa-inspired bathrooms provide comfort and seclusion, while the lower-level lounge with wet bar and media area offers flexible retreat space. A private ski room and wellness area with sauna complete the offering.

Direct ski-in/ski-out access to Big Sky Resort and Montage Big Sky's full ownership program—private concierge, world-class dining, spa, fitness, and curated outdoor adventures—define the lifestyle. A golf or ski/social membership is required; application, approval, and deposit are due no later than closing.
