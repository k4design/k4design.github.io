The Story

172 Ciel Drive offers a rare opportunity to own a private mountain retreat on 6.33 pristine acres in the heart of Big Sky, Montana. Positioned within one of the region's most sought-after residential enclaves, this 3,868 sq ft home delivers the full measure of the Montana high-country lifestyle—with direct access to Big Sky Resort's world-class skiing and an easy drive to Yellowstone National Park's north entrance.

The home balances mountain character with refined comfort, offering generous living spaces across 5 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms suited to families and entertaining guests across multiple seasons. Whether arriving for winter ski runs on more than 5,800 acres of terrain or summer hiking and fly-fishing along the Gallatin River, this property serves as a year-round base for the outdoor life that defines Big Sky.

Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport lies approximately 45 minutes away, offering direct flights to major US cities—making this mountain retreat as accessible as it is remote. The town of Big Sky provides a growing selection of dining, shops, and services year-round.
