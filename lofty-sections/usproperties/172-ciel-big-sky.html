The Story

172 Ciel Drive offers a rare opportunity to own a private mountain retreat on 1.31 pristine acres in the heart of Big Sky, Montana. Positioned within one of the region's most sought-after residential enclaves, this property delivers the full measure of the Montana high-country lifestyle—with direct access to Big Sky Resort's world-class skiing and an easy drive to Yellowstone National Park's north entrance.
