The Story

Penthouse #11 at The Brittany ascends to the absolute height of luxury in prestigious Park Shore—a commanding residence where the sky meets the shore. From the moment the keyed private elevator opens into the gracious foyer, unobstructed views of the Gulf of America and Venetian Bay unfold through expansive floor-to-ceiling glass walls. Soaring 10-foot ceilings and the rare combination of single-family estate scale with high-rise ease define a lifestyle of uncompromising grandeur.

The sprawling floor plan spans 6,543 sq ft of air-conditioned living across six spacious bedrooms plus a den and study, 7 baths, and a magnificent Grand Salon anchored by a fireplace—a rare find in beachfront condominium living. Vast wrap-around terraces offer panoramic vistas from sunrise to sunset, while the private rooftop sun deck stands as the crown jewel: an exclusive sanctuary for sunbathing or starlit gatherings available to no other unit in the building. The lavish primary suite features a sitting area, an adjacent study, and a spa-inspired bath framed by captivating water views. A private in-residence exercise room with sauna and exceptional storage throughout complete the offering.

Ownership includes a private poolside cabana and 3 deeded parking spaces. The Brittany delivers world-class building amenities—beachfront pool and spa, tennis courts, fitness center, guest suites, and 24-hour security—just a short stroll from the upscale dining and shopping of Venetian Village.
