The Story

A true penthouse estate in one of Naples' most coveted addresses, Penthouse #11 at The Brittany at Park Shore commands the building's highest floor with unobstructed panoramic views of the Gulf of Mexico and Venetian Bay—a perspective unavailable to the public for over 15 years. A keyed private elevator opens directly to a gracious foyer that leads into the Grand Salon, anchored by two fireplaces and walls of floor-to-ceiling glass that dissolve the boundary between interior and sea.

The residence unfolds across 6,543 sq ft of air-conditioned living with 10-foot ceilings throughout, six en-suite bedrooms, a den and study, a spa-inspired primary bath with private sitting area, and an in-residence exercise room with sauna. Wrap-around terraces and a private rooftop sun deck create an additional 2,805 sq ft of outdoor living space—among the largest private outdoor footprints in Park Shore.

The Brittany building delivers the full Naples luxury lifestyle: beachfront pool and spa, tennis courts, and 24-hour security. Three deeded parking spaces and a private poolside cabana complete a residence of extraordinary rarity in one of Southwest Florida's most enduring luxury communities.
