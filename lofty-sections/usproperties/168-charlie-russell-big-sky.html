The Story

168 Charlie Russell Loop is a Montana legacy home in the truest sense—an exquisitely artisan-crafted, hand-hewn log estate commanding 20 pristine acres within the gated enclave of Big EZ Estates. From its elevated vantage point, one of the most breathtaking in all of Big Sky, the residence delivers sweeping, cinematic views of Yellowstone National Park and the Spanish Peaks, where sunrises, moonrises, and alpenglow sunsets become part of daily life.

At 7,434 sq ft, the residence is designed for both grand entertaining and intimate retreat. The main level unfolds with soaring ceilings, a dramatic floor-to-ceiling stone fireplace, expansive living and dining spaces, and a gourmet kitchen outfitted with premium appliances. An executive office with private deck access sits just off the great room. Four private primary suites—each with its own fireplace, outdoor access, and individual climate control—offer hotel-level comfort, while a charming timber-frame bunk room with en-suite bath adds warmth for family gatherings. Six interior fireplaces, an outdoor fireplace, a landscaped fire pit, and a new hot tub complete the four-season experience.

The lower level is dedicated to recreation and relaxation: a spacious family room, wet bar, pool table, and additional fireplace open seamlessly to the outdoors. A wrap-around deck and multiple patios extend living into the landscape. With 20 acres of usable land, a 3-car garage, and the ability to obtain a golf or ski/social membership at Spanish Peaks Mountain Club, this is a legacy property of rare permanence and provenance.
