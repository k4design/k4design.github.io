The Story

168 Charlie Russell Loop is a Montana legacy home in the truest sense—an exquisitely artisan-crafted, hand-hewn log estate commanding 20 pristine acres within the gated enclave of Big EZ Estates. From its elevated position, the property delivers one of the most breathtaking panoramic vantage points in all of Big Sky, where mountain ranges define the horizon in every direction and the silence of the high country is broken only by wind and wildlife.

Built in 2007 to the highest standard of Montana craftsmanship, the 7,434 sq ft main residence is a study in the art of log construction: hand-selected and hand-hewn timbers, soaring interior volumes, and a material palette drawn from the land itself. Five bedrooms, six baths, and a 3-car garage serve a floor plan designed for both intimate family living and generous entertaining across multiple seasons.

The property provides the ability to obtain a golf or ski/social membership at Spanish Peaks Mountain Club, opening access to two championship golf courses, tennis, fitness, fine dining, and ski-in/ski-out terrain. Big Sky Resort, Yellowstone National Park, and Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport are all within reach—making this 20-acre legacy holding as connected as it is private.
