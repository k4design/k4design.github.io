The Story

Designed to accommodate up to 36 guests in uncompromising luxury, 1148 Grand Traverse Pkwy is an extraordinary 16-bedroom estate set within the gates of Reunion Resort—one of Central Florida's most prestigious planned communities. Built in 2021 with no expense spared, this property redefines what a private residence can offer at scale.

Grand interiors feature soaring ceilings, high-end finishes, and expansive living spaces throughout. The chef's kitchen is equipped with top-of-the-line commercial appliances, stunning stone countertops, a large center island with seating for 12, and two commercial-sized refrigerators and freezers. An adjacent dining area accommodates 20. All 16 bedrooms are individually themed with spa-inspired en-suite bathrooms—including a Star Wars-themed room with 12 bunk beds and a dedicated gaming loft.

Entertainment venues span multiple levels: a dedicated spa with sauna, professional in-home gym, professional-grade golf simulator, half-court indoor basketball court, two-lane bowling alley, arcade and game room, and a private 12-seat cinema. Outside, the resort-caliber grounds deliver a summer kitchen, pool with water slide, winding lazy river, and hot tub—all within Reunion Resort, which offers three signature golf courses and full resort services just minutes from Walt Disney World.
