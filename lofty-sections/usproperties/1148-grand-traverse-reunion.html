The Story

1148 Grand Traverse Pkwy is not a house—it is a resort unto itself. Built in 2021 within the gates of Reunion Resort, this extraordinary 16-bedroom, 20-bath mega-estate was purpose-engineered to accommodate 36 guests in uncompromising luxury, with a proven vacation rental track record that sets it apart as both a trophy asset and a performing investment.

The commercial-grade chef's kitchen defines the scale: top-of-the-line appliances, stunning stone countertops, a center island with seating for 12, two commercial-sized refrigerators and freezers, and an adjacent dining area for 20. All 16 bedrooms are individually themed with spa-inspired en-suite baths—including a Star Wars-themed bunk room with 12 bunks, a dedicated gaming loft, and themed décor from floor to ceiling.

The entertainment program rivals any five-star resort: a 12-seat private cinema, two-lane bowling alley (bowling shoes included in every size), professional golf simulator, half-court indoor basketball, dedicated arcade and game room, full in-home gym, and a private spa suite with sauna. Outside, the resort-caliber grounds deliver a summer kitchen, pool with water slide, winding lazy river, and hot tub. Reunion Resort itself wraps the property in three signature golf courses—Jack Nicklaus, Tom Watson, and Arnold Palmer designs—along with full concierge services, all just minutes from Walt Disney World.
