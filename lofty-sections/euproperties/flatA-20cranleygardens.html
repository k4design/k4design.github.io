The Story

A bright large one bedroom apartment with private patio in one of the most desired streets in South Kensington. The property comprises a large reception room, an excellent kitchen, bathroom and a large bedroom with direct access to a private patio. The property is a beautiful white stucco building and is located in the heart of South Kensington.

Cranley Gardens benefits from the amenities of Gloucester Road and features access to the wonderful communal gardens opposite. The street is ideally positioned between the Fulham Road and Old Brompton Road—a wonderful location with Kings Road, Chelsea, Kensington and Gloucester Road minutes away. South Kensington with all its excellent amenities and transport links is on the doorstep, and Hyde Park is within a 10 minute walk.

It would make an excellent home and pied-à-terre and a wonderful rental investment if desired.
