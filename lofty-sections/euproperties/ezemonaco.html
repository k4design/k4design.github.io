The Story

Introducing an extraordinary villa in the prestigious enclave of Èze-Bord-de-Mer, offering approximately 570 m² of elegant living space. Meticulously restored to preserve its historic character, the residence showcases remarkable period details, including original Venetian mosaic flooring and preserved frescoes. Refined interiors are designed for both comfort and entertaining, featuring a professional chef's kitchen, formal dining room, and a spectacular primary suite with a luxurious en-suite bath and private dressing area.

Expansive windows and terraces invite natural light throughout the home, seamlessly blending indoor and outdoor living. Every space has been thoughtfully curated to balance timeless elegance with modern comfort. Set within 2,933 m² of landscaped grounds, the estate captures panoramic Mediterranean Sea views while offering rare privacy just moments from the beach. A newly rebuilt guest house, ideal as a music or art studio, provides stylish independent accommodation. Additional highlights include a renovated swimming pool with pool house, two newly constructed garages, a private historic chapel, and a greenhouse—creating a timeless Riviera retreat near Monaco and Nice Airport.

For further details, brochure or a private viewing, please contact the listing agents.
