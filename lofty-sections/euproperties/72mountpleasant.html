The Story

Welcome to Mount Pleasant, Aspley Guise—where countryside charm meets modern living. Tucked away in the sought-after village of Aspley Guise, this unique split-level detached home is all about style, space, and stunning views. With two incredible roof terraces, flexible living areas, and a backdrop of open countryside, this is a home that feels like a retreat—yet sits moments from everything you need.

Step inside to a bright, welcoming reception hall that flows into a sleek modern kitchen complete with granite worktops and built-in appliances. From here, the open layout connects effortlessly to the dining and living area, where you'll find a feature fireplace, a light-filled sunroom, and doors out to a terrace overlooking the rolling hills—perfect for a morning coffee or evening glass of wine.

The versatile lower ground floor adds even more to love. Currently used as a family room, it comes complete with its own en suite and private entrance, making it ideal as a guest suite, fourth bedroom, home office, or even a self-contained studio for older teens or visiting relatives. Upstairs, you'll find three generous bedrooms and a stylish family bathroom featuring a freestanding roll-top bath. The master suite takes things up another level, with bi-fold doors leading to a private roof terrace that frames those incredible countryside views, plus a modern en suite for ultimate comfort.

Outside, the rear garden is beautifully landscaped with raised flower beds, mature trees, and a sunny patio seating area. The front offers driveway parking, and there's plenty of on-street parking available too. Aspley Guise is one of Bedfordshire's most desirable villages, offering a welcoming community, local pub, primary school, pre-school, and golf course. The neighbouring Woburn Sands brings charming cafés, boutique shops, and restaurants—while Central Milton Keynes and key road links (M1, A5) are only a short drive away.
