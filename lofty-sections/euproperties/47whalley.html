The Story

Welcome to Whalley Drive, a home that's been treated to a top-to-bottom glow-up—think pastel tones, high-end villa vibes, and an atmosphere that just feels good. Downstairs is where the magic happens: a bright open-plan kitchen, living, and dining space designed for dinner parties, Sunday pancakes, and everything in between. When you need a little peace, the formal lounge has you covered—perfect for Netflix marathons or pretending you're reading that book you bought six months ago. There's also a dedicated home office, so you can work hard (or look like you are) without leaving the house.

Upstairs, you'll find four spacious bedrooms, including a master suite with a walk-in wardrobe that'll finally give your clothes the space they deserve, and a sleek ensuite that feels straight out of a boutique hotel. Outside, the garden is your private escape, complete with a versatile outbuilding—ideal for a home gym, games room, or somewhere to hide from the kids.

All this sits in a prime Central Milton Keynes location, just moments from shops, dining, green spaces, and transport links. So yes—you can have villa-style living and still pop into M&S in five minutes. Whalley Drive: modern comfort, a touch of luxury, and just the right amount of fun.
