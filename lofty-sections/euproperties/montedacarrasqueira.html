The Story

Set in the countryside of São Domingos e Vale de Água, this restored Alentejo quinta offers a lifestyle defined by space, rhythm, and discretion, with easy access to the coast and the wider Alentejo region. Positioned on a 3,290 m² plot, the house provides 102 m² of comfortable, low-maintenance living, ideal for year-round use or extended stays.

Two bedrooms, including a suite, are arranged around light-filled interiors designed for simplicity and ease. Outdoor living is central to the experience, with a private swimming pool, fruit trees, and open views creating a natural setting for entertaining, gathering with friends and family, and enjoying long Alentejo evenings.

Modern comforts are quietly integrated, including reversible air conditioning, double glazing, a fireplace, and solar panels for water heating. The location balances privacy and accessibility, with Porto Covo and Vila Nova de Milfontes within easy reach, along with golf courses and Melides' understated international scene. The property has also been successfully operated as a short-term rental, offering flexibility without compromising its residential character. This is not a house designed to impress; it is a home designed to make sense over time.
