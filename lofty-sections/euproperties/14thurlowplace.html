The Story

A stunning interior designed family home in the heart of South Kensington in one of the most iconic locations. The house has been refurbished throughout to an exacting level and is drenched in light and has the most wonderful garden at the back which is an utter oasis of tranquility.

The property benefits from 5 double bedrooms, 4 bathrooms and a WC. The property has wonderful family living space and the principal bedroom is located on the first floor with large French windows and wonderful views over to the Victoria and Albert museum. The property is extremely quiet throughout. The property is laid out extremely well optimizing all the space to its best advantage.

This property would make a wonderful family home. Equally it would make a fantastic rental investment. The house is located moments from South Kensington and all the transport links and wonderful amenities and benefits from walking distance to Knightsbridge and Chelsea.
