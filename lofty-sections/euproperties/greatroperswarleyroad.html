The Story

Great Ropers is a substantial Country Estate within walking distance of Brentwood Station and Town Centre. Set within 5.8 acres of manicured grounds, the property provides over 8,000 sq. ft. of accommodation, featuring five double bedrooms (three with en-suites), five reception rooms, a bespoke cinema room, Tom Howley kitchen, and an impressive leisure complex and triple secure garaging.

The residence combines generous living space with high-specification finishes. The Tom Howley kitchen forms the heart of the home, while five reception rooms offer flexibility for both family life and entertaining. The bespoke cinema room provides a dedicated space for film and media, and the leisure complex adds a resort-style dimension to everyday living.

Outside, the 5.8-acre grounds are manicured and private, with an infinity pool, extensive garaging and a setting that feels both accessible—close to Brentwood’s amenities and transport—and distinctly rural. Ideal for a family seeking a country estate with excellent connectivity to London and the wider region.
