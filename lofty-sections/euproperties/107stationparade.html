The Story

Constructed in 2021 and set on the edge of the Stray, this modern two-bedroom apartment offers convenience, style, and practicality in a sought-after central location. The property is ideal for professionals and young couples, with the town centre and its amenities just a short walk away. Frequent commuters will also benefit from excellent transport links close at hand.

For investors, this apartment represents a strong opportunity, with consistently high rental demand in the area and a tenant in situ until March 2026 with the option to extend. The accommodation features a well-presented open-plan living, dining, and kitchen area, enjoying natural light from dual-aspect windows and a Juliet balcony. Sound reducing glazing provides a quieter environment, enhancing comfort.

There are two double bedrooms, including a generous principal suite with its own Juliet balcony and a large en-suite. Both the en-suite and main bathroom are fitted with high-quality fixtures and benefit from underfloor heating. Residents enjoy access to well-maintained communal areas, elevator access to all floors, secure entry via intercom, and the additional benefit of an allocated parking space with EV Charging.
