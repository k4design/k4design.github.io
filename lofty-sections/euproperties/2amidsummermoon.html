The Story

2A Midsummer Moon is a 6 bedroom, 6 bathroom, gated home in a very exclusive area in Wavendon, sitting on a 1 acre plot. With 5,327 sq ft of living space, this property offers exceptional scale and privacy in one of Milton Keynes’ most sought-after locations.

Cross End and Wavendon provide a peaceful setting while remaining well connected to Milton Keynes centre, major roads and London. The house is designed for modern family living and entertaining, with standout amenities including a home gym, Pilates studio, swimming pool and games room.

The 1 acre garden provides ample outdoor space for relaxation and recreation. With six bedrooms and six bathrooms, the layout suits large families or those who regularly host guests, all within a secure, gated setting.
