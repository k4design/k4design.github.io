The Story

Hazelhurst Farm is a rare opportunity in the New Forest, a beautifully refurbished residential and equestrian estate extending to around nine acres, discreetly positioned on the peaceful edge of Sway. Close to 10,000 sq ft is arranged across the main farmhouse, a collection of character cottages, an impressive events barn, new stables, paddocks, a floodlit riding arena and a private tennis court. The estate suits a refined family home, multi-generational living or a lifestyle business with proven income.

Sway offers everyday convenience with village shops, schools, a tennis club and a mainline station with direct services to London Waterloo in under 70 minutes. Lymington, with its marinas and boutiques, is nearby, while Brockenhurst provides further amenities and a popular golf course.

The main farmhouse blends period charm with a crisp contemporary finish. A welcoming hallway with fireplace leads to a generous sitting room and a chef's kitchen with bespoke cabinetry and premium appliances. Bi-fold doors open from the dining area to the gardens, ideal for relaxed entertaining. Upstairs are three calm bedrooms with leafy views, including a bright dual-aspect principal suite with excellent storage. A range of charming cottages offers flexibility for guests, extended family or income. The events barn is a standout feature with soaring timber ceilings, a bar, pizza oven and smoker, suited to private events or commercial use. Equestrian facilities include a new stable block, multiple paddocks and a floodlit arena, complemented by lawns and a private tennis court.
