The Story

Set on the ever-popular Berrymead Gardens, this four-bedroom Victorian terraced house offers a relaxed mix of character, space, and everyday comfort. With its classic frontage and friendly residential feel, it's a home that's easy to settle into and enjoy.

The ground floor is centred around a bright and spacious double reception room, where high ceilings and large windows create a light, comfortable atmosphere. It's a flexible area that works well for both quiet nights in and entertaining friends, with plenty of room to relax. At the rear, the kitchen and dining area is a practical, sociable space with good storage and worktop space, opening directly onto the private garden. The garden is ideal for outdoor dining, summer evenings, or simply unwinding at the end of the day.

The first floor offers well-proportioned bedrooms and a family bathroom, while the loft has been thoughtfully converted to create an additional bedroom with its own ensuite shower room. This top-floor space works particularly well as a main bedroom, guest suite, or private home office, adding extra flexibility to the layout. Berrymead Gardens is well located for excellent transport links, with Acton Central (London Overground) 500m, South Acton (London Overground) 600m and Acton Town (District and Piccadilly) 800m, all within easy reach. Surrounded by parks, good schools, and a great selection of local shops, cafés, and restaurants, this is a comfortable and well-connected home in a popular West London neighbourhood. Offered chain free and with vacant possession.
