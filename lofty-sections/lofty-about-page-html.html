Our Story

Founded with a vision to transform the luxury real estate landscape, Aperture Global emerged from a recognition that the industry needed a new standard of excellence. Our founder, Robert Palmer, brings decades of industry experience and a proven track record of building successful real estate brands that empower agents and deliver exceptional results.

The purpose behind launching Aperture Global was clear: to create a brokerage that combines cutting-edge technology, world-class marketing capabilities, and unparalleled service to serve the most discerning clients in the world's most prestigious markets. We operate with a presence concentrated on the greatest luxury cities around the world, spanning 6 continents and 20+ countries.

Today, Aperture Global stands as the first independent luxury brokerage to have a global launch at its inception, built on a battle-tested, performance-driven platform that enables us to scale and maintain operational efficiencies from day one.

Leadership Team

Robert Palmer

Founder & CEO

Robert Palmer is a serial entrepreneur, business leader and marketing guru who has built an impressive portfolio of brands and businesses through innovation, hard work, and a relentless passion for success. In 2022, Palmer launched LPT Realty, the fastest growing single-entity national brokerage in history, designed to empower agents through an innovative model that focuses on agent choice. Seeing a hole in the industry for truly global and effective marketing for luxury real estate, he launched Aperture in 2025, further disrupting the industry.

Michael Valdes

Global President

Michael Valdes is a leader in luxury real estate, serving as Global President of Aperture and CEO of LPT International. Prior to this role, he served as Chief Growth Officer of exp Realty and former Senior Vice President of Realogy Corporation where he spent 15 years at Sotheby's Realty growing that brand to over 70 countries. Prior to real estate, Valdes spent 10 years at Deutsche Bank Private Banking.
