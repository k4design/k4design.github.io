Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about luxury real estate marketing and media strategy.

What makes your targeting different?
I use privacy-safe household-level addressable TV and CTV targeting combined with premium audio, reaching only verified high-net-worth households. This precision eliminates waste and ensures your message reaches qualified prospects.
How do you measure success?
Every campaign includes incremental reach measurement, view-through rates, qualified site visits, and high-intent actions. I provide detailed reporting on campaign performance and audience engagement with full attribution tracking.
Do you offer market exclusivity?
Yes, I respect non-compete windows and typically work with one luxury property per market at a time. This ensures your property receives undivided attention and prevents market saturation.
How quickly can we launch?
Typical launch timeline is 2-3 weeks from contract signing, including audience modeling, creative adaptation, and inventory placement. Rush launches can be accommodated for urgent campaigns.
What do engagements cost?
Investment ranges from $50K to $500K+ depending on market, property value, and campaign scope. I provide detailed proposals with clear ROI projections and performance guarantees.
Do you manage creative and production?
Yes, I work with a network of luxury real estate photographers, videographers, and creative directors to ensure your property is presented at the highest level. All creative is optimized for CTV and audio platforms.
