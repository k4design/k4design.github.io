Property Overview

This exclusive waterfront villa represents the ultimate in Monaco luxury living. Perched on the prestigious French Riviera, this extraordinary estate offers direct access to the Mediterranean Sea and panoramic views of the iconic Monte Carlo skyline. The property features a private marina, helipad, and over 18,000 square feet of meticulously designed living space.

The villa showcases the finest European craftsmanship and materials, including Carrara marble, custom bronze fixtures, and hand-painted frescoes. The expansive terraces and gardens create the perfect environment for both intimate gatherings and grand entertaining, while the private beach access provides exclusive Mediterranean experiences.
