Every Aperture Global routed seller flows through the same four-phase sequence — a coordinated arc of pre-market, launch, syndication, and sustained amplification. Designed to outperform conventional listing strategy at every interval.

01
Phase One
Pre-Market  ·  Days −14 → −1

First Look — before the market knows.

The property is marketed utilizing Zillow Preview, promotional alerts, and the entire Aperture Global agent network — generating buzz and buyer interest before the listing ever hits the MLS.

Zillow Preview is an exclusive pre-market feature that allows your listing to appear on Zillow before it goes live on the MLS. During the preview window, millions of active Zillow buyers can discover, save, and request information on the property — building a queue of interested buyers before day one. The listing displays a countdown to the public go-live date, creating urgency and amplifying first-day momentum in a way traditional MLS launch alone cannot replicate.
